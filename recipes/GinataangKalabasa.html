GinataangKalabasa


Description

Ginataang Kalabasa with Longanisa is a savory Filipino dish that combines the creamy richness of coconut milk with the sweet and nutty flavors of kalabasa (squash) and the savory, slightly sweet taste of longanisa. Its composed primarily of diced kalabasa, coconut milk and minced longanisa meat, sans the skin. This dish is known for its creamy texture and the delightful blend of flavors from the coconut milk and longanisa, with a hint of sweetness from the kalabasa.

The dish is typically served hot and pairs perfectly with steamed rice, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. It can also be complemented with side dishes like sautéed greens or a simple salad to balance the richness of the main dish.


Ingredients

  • Diced kalabasa (squash)
  • Coconut milk
  • Garlic, minced
  • Minced longanisa meat (skin removed)
  • Onion, chopped (optional)
  • Fish sauce or salt, to taste
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Sauté Aromatics: Heat a bit of cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic (and chopped onion if using) and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Cook Longanisa: Add the minced longanisa meat to the pan. Cook until it’s browned and cooked through.
  3. Add Kalabasa: Add the diced kalabasa to the pan. Stir well to combine with the longanisa.
  4. Pour Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk. Stir to mix everything together.
  5. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Cook until the kalabasa is tender and the sauce has thickened, about 15-20 minutes.
  6. Season: Season with fish sauce or salt to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice.
