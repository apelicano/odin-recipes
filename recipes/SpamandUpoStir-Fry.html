Spam and Upo Stir-Fry with Eggs


Description

Spam and Upo Stir-Fry with Eggs is a quick and delicious dish that combines the savory taste of diced Spam with the mild, refreshing flavor of upo (bottle gourd). Beaten eggs add a creamy texture, while garlic and optional onions provide a fragrant base. This stir-fry is best served hot with steamed rice, making it a perfect meal for busy evenings.


Ingredients

  • 1 can of Spam, diced
  • 1 medium upo (bottle gourd), peeled and sliced
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • A splash of soy sauce (optional)
  • Half a teaspoon of chicken powder
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Dice the Spam, peel and slice the upo, and beat the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: Heat a bit of cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the minced garlic (and chopped onion if using) and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Cook Spam: Add the diced Spam to the pan. Cook until it’s browned and slightly crispy.
  4. Add Upo and Seasonings: Add the sliced upo to the pan. Stir well to combine with the Spam. Add a splash of soy sauce or a tablespoon of oyster sauce, and cook until the upo is tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add Eggs: Push the Spam and upo to one side of the pan. Pour the beaten eggs into the empty space and scramble until they are just set.
  6. Combine and Finish: Mix everything together in the pan. Season with chicken powder, salt, and pepper to taste. Drizzle with sesame oil before serving.
  7. Serve: Serve hot with steamed rice.
